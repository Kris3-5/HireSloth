About HireSloth

HireSloth is a privacy-first job board built to protect job seekers and provide transparent listings for employers. Unlike traditional platforms, we never sell or misuse personal data.

Our goal is to make job hunting stress-free, honest, and effective. We encourage employers to share clear details about their openings including job responsibilities, salary ranges, and company information.

HireSloth automatically removes expired listings after 30 days, and allows users to report duplicates, scams, or errors to ensure only high-quality jobs remain visible.
